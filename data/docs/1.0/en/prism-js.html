Changing Your Theme

To change your default theme go to:

  1. Prism js download page. And click on a theme you like on the right side of the page.

  1. Then you need to click on the download css button as seen in the following image.

  1. Finally copy your new prism.css file and paste it to /src/css/ folder in your olodoc-boostrap template generator project.
